Slide 10:
Title: What is Configuration Management - 2?
Contents:
- Configuration management is the process of:Identifying and defining configuration items (CIs) of a system;Controlling release and change of CIs throughout the lifecycle;Recording and reporting status of CIs and change requests;Verifying completeness and correctness of Cis;

Slide 11:
Title: What is Configuration Management - 3?
Contents:
- Change Control: the set of formal procedures which record and enable changes to configuration items.
