Slide 7:
Title: The Agile Manifesto
Contents:
- “We are uncovering better ways of developing software by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work we have come to value:

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
Working software over comprehensive documentation
Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
Responding to change over following a plan
That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.”
www.agilemanifesto.org (2001)

Slide 8:
Title: What is The Waterfall Model?
Contents:
- The Waterfall Model is a sequential development model.
It insists on each stage of the development process to be completed before moving on to the next step.

Slide 9:
Title: What is DSDM?

- Dynamic Systems Development Method (DSDM) Agile Project Framework (2014 Onwards)

Any kind of project
Focused on business benefit
On-time and in budget
Quality and rigour
Incremental
Collaborative

Slide 24:
Title: What is a Timebox?
Contents:
- A timebox is a fixed period of time, at the end of which an objective has been met.

The time available dictates work done

The aim of a timebox is to make something useful

A timebox is product-focused, not task-focused
